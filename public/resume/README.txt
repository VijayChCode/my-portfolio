RESUME UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
==========================

This folder is reserved for storing your resume PDF file.

To upload your resume:
1. Click the "Admin Login" button at the bottom of the portfolio
2. Enter the admin password
3. Use the "Upload Resume (PDF)" file input
4. Select your resume PDF file
5. A floating "Resume" button will appear at the bottom right
6. Visitors can click it to download your resume

Supported Format: PDF only (.pdf)

The resume will be available for download immediately after upload.
